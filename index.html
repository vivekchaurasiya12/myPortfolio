About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm Vivek Kumar Chaurasiya, an intern-level student with a passion for web development. With expertise in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and ReactJS, I craft dynamic and engaging websites that leave a lasting impression.

As a dedicated learner, I constantly strive to expand my skill set and stay up-to-date with the latest industry trends. Alongside my technical abilities, I possess strong English proficiency, allowing me to effectively communicate and collaborate with clients and colleagues.
